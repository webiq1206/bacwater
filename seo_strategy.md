# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages
- Public educational content under `/learn/**`
- Public tool pages under `/tools/**`
- Public product and catalog pages under `/shop/**`, `/buy`, and `/peptides/**`
- Public trust and policy pages such as `/about`, `/faq`, `/contact`, `/terms`, `/privacy`, `/disclaimer`, `/shipping-returns`, and `/editorial-policy`

## Out of scope
- Authenticated or user-specific plan pages under `/plan/[id]/**`
- Account creation and sign-in pages under `/signin` and `/signup`
- Checkout flow pages such as `/cart`, `/checkout`, and `/checkout/success`
- Admin pages under `/admin/**`
- API routes except where they directly power crawlability artifacts such as sitemap, robots, `llms.txt`, or OG image generation

## Target audience
- People researching peptide reconstitution and BAC water usage for laboratory or educational purposes
- Buyers evaluating BAC water supplies, calculators, and research support content

- peptide dosing calculator
