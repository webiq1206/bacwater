# BACwater.ai - Page Quality Scorecard

**Date:** 2026-07-02

Each page/template is scored 0-100 as the average of eight dimensions (each 0-100):

1. **Content** - depth and uniqueness
2. **Keyword** - keyword and intent match (title, H1, body)
3. **Meta** - title and description quality and length compliance
4. **Schema** - JSON-LD coverage and correctness
5. **EEAT** - experience, expertise, authority, trust signals
6. **Links** - internal linking (in and out)
7. **AEO** - answer extractability (direct answers, FAQ, HTML-rendered Q&A)
8. **UX/CWV** - layout, readability, Core Web Vitals posture

Grade bands: A 90-100, B 80-89, C 70-79, D 60-69, F under 60.

## Lowest Scorers - Prioritize These

1. **`/about` (74, C)** - thinnest strategic page. Add depth: who runs it, how calculations are verified, why company-level authorship, links out to `/editorial-policy`, `/learn`, `/tools`, `/faq`. Biggest EEAT+content lift for the domain's trust profile.
2. **`/privacy` (74, C)** - align H1 to "Privacy Policy", expand to cover analytics (GA + Clarity are loaded), data retention, and processor disclosure. Low effort, removes a thin-content flag.
3. **`/contact` (75, C)** - add a "check the FAQ or browse guides first" cross-link block above the form (currently near-zero outbound links). Lifts internal linking and AEO.
4. **`/tools` (79, C)** - add an ItemList of the 6 calculators, a one-line direct answer per tool, and a keyword-bearing H1. Weakest AEO (66) among indexable pages.
5. **`/terms` and `/disclaimer` (76, C)** - fine as legal pages; add a short "back to guides / plan / shop" footer link to raise internal-linking floor. Low priority.
6. **`/learn/[slug]` (79, C)** - add `WebPageJsonLd`, FAQPage where guides carry Q&A, and in-body cross-links to matching tools/peptides. Rendering only supports bold/italic/paragraphs, so add heading structure for scannability and AEO.
7. **`/tools/mg-to-mcg` (79, C)** - terse H1 and a 60-char title with no headroom; add a direct-answer sentence and SoftwareApplication schema.

### Cross-cutting quick wins (lift most pages a few points each)
- Add `SoftwareApplication`/`WebApplication` schema to all 6 calculators and `/plan` (raises Schema on 7 pages).
- Trim the 7 over-length meta descriptions and 4 over-length titles (raises Meta across the board).
- Replace soft H1s (`/shop`, `/tools`, `/plan/new`, `/tools/dose`, `/tools/supplies`) with keyword-bearing ones (raises Keyword + AEO).
- Add `WebPageJsonLd` to `/`, `/learn/[slug]`, `/shop/[slug]`.

### What is already excellent (protect it)
- Per-peptide pages, comparisons, and `/buy` are A-grade: answer-first lede, HTML-rendered FAQ accordions, HowTo/Product/FAQPage/ImageObject schema, breadcrumbs, and dense internal linking. These are the AEO and citation engine of the site. Keep the "Last reviewed {month}" freshness stamp and the single-BreadcrumbList discipline intact.
