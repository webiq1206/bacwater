## Cannibalization Watch

| Risk | Pages | Verdict / Resolution |
|------|-------|----------------------|
| "buy bac water" family | `/buy` vs `/shop` vs `/shop/bac-water` PDPs | RESOLVED by intent split. `/buy` = editorial commercial landing ("buy bacteriostatic water online", answer-first, ships/near-me FAQ). `/shop` = catalog listing ("buy bac water, syringes and supplies"). PDPs = single-SKU transactional. Distinct titles, distinct H1s. Keep, but ensure `/buy` links down to PDPs (it does) and PDPs do not also try to rank for "buy bacteriostatic water online". Monitor: `/buy` and the primary bac-water PDP both emit Product schema for the same catalog item, which is acceptable but should stay the ONE featured product on `/buy`, not all of them. |
| "how much bac water" family | `/tools/bac-water` vs `/plan` vs `/tools/reconstitution` | RESOLVED by depth/intent. `/tools/bac-water` = single-answer calc ("how much bac water to add", informational). `/plan` = full transactional plan builder with PDF/label. `/tools/reconstitution` = full multi-output calculator (transactional). Distinct primaries, no overlap. Peptide pages own "how much bac water for [peptide]" which the generic tools do not target. |
| "reconstitution calculator" | `/plan` vs `/tools/reconstitution` | RESOLVED. `/plan` primary = "peptide reconstitution plan builder"; `/tools/reconstitution` primary = "peptide reconstitution calculator". Planner vs calculator, transactional both but different query. Keep distinct. |
| "syringe units" duplicate | `/tools/syringe-units` vs `/tools/ml-to-units` | RESOLVED. `/tools/ml-to-units` now 308-redirects to `/tools/syringe-units`; only the canonical page is indexable and in the sitemap. |
| "peptide reconstitution" broad head | `/` vs `/plan` vs `/learn` vs `/peptides` | RESOLVED by role. `/` = navigational hub around "bac water calculator". `/plan` = transactional planner. `/learn` = informational hub. `/peptides` = per-peptide calculator hub. Four different long-tail primaries under one broad theme. No head-term collision. |
| "bac water vs sterile water" | `/learn/vs/sterile-water` vs `/faq` | LOW. FAQ mentions the difference in one answer; the comparison page owns the query with a table, infographic, and FAQ. FAQ should not add a dedicated "vs sterile water" H2 that competes. Currently fine. |
